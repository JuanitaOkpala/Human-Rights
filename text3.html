The Impact of Discrimination Based on Beauty Ideologies

What is discrimination?

Discrimination is the unfair or prejudicial treatment of people and groups based on characteristics such as race, gender, age, or sexual orientation. That’s the simple answer. (American Psychological Association).

It can manifest in various forms, including verbal or physical abuse, exclusion, or denial of opportunities, and can have detrimental effects on the well-being and social standing of those affected. Discrimination goes against the principles of equality, fairness, and human rights and can hinder the progress of a diverse and inclusive society.

Emotional, mental, and psychological effects of discrimination include: Low self-esteem and self- doubt, Anxiety and depression, feelings and inadequacy and worthlessness.

Understanding Beauty: A Complicated, Controversial, and Assorted Concept

Understanding beauty as an assorted topic involves recognizing and accepting its diverse or different dimensions to which its can be fully appreciated. Acknowledging its complexities and developing a more nuanced idea about it.

What is beauty?

Beauty is a subjective and complex concept that varies across cultures, individuals, and time periods. It generally refers to a combination of qualities that are perceived as pleasing, attractive, or aesthetically appealing. Beauty can be found in various aspects of life, such as art, nature, people, and even abstract ideas. It often involves a sense of balance, harmony, and proportion, as well as the evocation of positive emotions and feelings. Beauty is often associated with the appreciation of aesthetics, and it can be experienced through various senses like sight, hearing, touch, taste, and smell. Beauty is influenced by history, culture and individuals (they're up bringing) - recently over the years it has been dominated tremendously by the media and other social platforms.

Human Rights

To raise awareness on the effects of discrimination as a result of different ideologies of what beauty is or is not

Authors:Juanita, Maaliyah, Abena, Maame Owusuaa, Kylis

In today's world beauty is often shaped by different ideologies, leading to different takes, opinions and perspectives on this sensitive topic. These varying ideas on beauty is cause the unfortunate discrimination for individuals who do not conform to these expectations. This presentation aims to raise awareness about the importance of embracing diverse perspectives on beauty and fostering a more inclusive society.

  • Understand Beauty
  • The Impact of Discrimination Based on Beauty Ideologies
  • Promoting Inclusivity, Acceptance and Practical Steps Towards Change
